About me

I am a PhD graduate from Télécom Paris (Institut Polytechnique de Paris), where I worked in collaboration with the CEA (Commissariat à l'Énergie Atomique et aux Énergies Alternatives) on deep learning methods for Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imaging. I defended my thesis Deep learning for SAR image enhancement: multi-modal fusion and interferometric denoising in 2025.
